Performance

GET YOUR HEART RACING

This engaging sports coupe embodies all the power, agility, and excitement of expert BMW M engineering.
Be careful – driving the All-New BMW M4 Coupe may be addictive.

High-precision engines.

The finely engineered 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo
inline-6 engine in the high-performance M4 Coupe can
unleash intense power – up to 503 horsepower in the
Competition model, taking you from 0-60 mph in 3.8
seconds

Manual or automatic
transmission.

Purists will love the manual transmission that comes as
standard on the BMW M4 Coupe, while drivers in the M4
Competition model enjoy the automatic precision of an
8-speed M STEPTRONIC transmission.

Advanced differential and
suspension.

With the standard M Sport Differential and Adaptive M
Suspension, you'll be able to handle the most challenging
curves and corners with split-second precision and
incredible agility.

High-powered M brakes.

Go from 60 to standstill in seconds with the standard M
Compound brakes with red, blue, or
black calipers. Or, upgrade your stopping power with available
M Carbon Ceramic brakes with standout gold calipers.

Design

THE SHOWSTOPPER

Coupe drivers know that looks matter – and the All-New BMW M4 Coupe is pure style, with performance
and pride built into every detail.

Attention-grabbing coupe design.

A distinctive, frameless, vertical kidney grille is the centerpiece of the BMW M4 Coupe's front end. The sculpted hood and available Icon Adaptive LED headlights with Laserlight strengthen the dramatic first impression.

Aerodynamics by M.

A standard carbon fiber roof, with fins for optimal airflow, lowers the coupe's weight and center of gravity. The M mirrors and side gills, also included as standard, further enhances the look of look of this high-performance sports car while reducing wind resistance.

Downforce and drag.

Keep your high-performance coupe cutting through the air with the stylish and effective standard rear diffuser and trunk spoiler. L-shaped LED taillights also sport a fresh design on the All-New BMW M4 Coupe.

Sport driver's seating.

Standard M Sport seats with illuminated M badges are comfortable on long drives, and provide optimal lateral support when you're putting the M4 Coupe through its paces.
